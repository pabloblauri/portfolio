A bit about me.

I have been involved in data and web development since 2022. I work remotely as a freelance data analyst.


My journey in the world of data began as a curiosity and has turned into an odyssey for a deep understanding of the information that resides within the numbers.


Data analysis and programming are some techniques that I am using to achieve my goals. My job consists in helping companies to clean, analyze and visualize their datasets.
